Services

At JGP Consultancy, we help companies organize and visualize their energy data. By providing expert advice and implementing automated data flows, we make it easier for organizations to comply with CSRD reporting requirements and the ISO 50001 standard. Our approach combines technical expertise in data management and dashboards with hands-on implementation, ensuring that businesses are not only compliant, but also empowered to make smarter decisions based on their energy performance.

Energy Data Quickscan

Get a clear picture of your company’s energy data in just a few days. With our Quickscan, we identify where your data comes from, how reliable it is, and where inefficiencies exist. You’ll receive a concise report with actionable steps to move towards CSRD and ISO 50001 compliance — without committing to a large project.

Implementation & Automation

Stop wasting time on manual data collection. We streamline and automate your energy data flows by connecting meters, production lines, and business systems into one central source. With real-time dashboards in tools like Power BI or Grafana, your data becomes reliable, transparent, and ready for audits and decision-making.

Compliance

Stay in control of your compliance process. We provide the knowledge, tools, and training your team needs to prepare accurate CSRD and ISO 50001 reports. With practical templates and expert guidance, your company builds internal expertise — ensuring efficient and error-free reporting, without depending on external parties.
